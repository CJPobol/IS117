What is "Radiant Night?"

Radiant Night follows an average citizen from a distant planet that also happens to contain human beings! What makes The Haeven different from Earth however, is their incredible technological advancements. Each citizen of this planet is suited with a small chip at birth that improves their general quality of life on the planet, as well as providing them with specific superhuman skillsets. These skills give each person a perfectly curated place in society. Our main character feels as though their place in society isn't cut out for them, and they soon find themself in serious trouble with the government for disobeying their purpose. Due to their disobedience, the main character is exiled to another human-suitable planet: Earth.

Radiant Night is a concept for a turn based RPG that I have been conceptualizing for quite some time now. This website serves to provide you with eerything you need to know about the game and its story. Learn about the major characters from the game, as well as notable locations where the story and gameplay action will take place. The game's detailed combat system also has a dedicated page for information. To get in contact with me about this project, feel free to fill out the form under "Contact!"
