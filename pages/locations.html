    Characters Locations

Charlie and Jacke's House

This is where the game begins, and it serves as somewhat of a home base for players in the early stages of the game. This is where the player is first led upon their arrival on Earth by the two siblings, and it quickly becomes a welcoming home.

Downtown

An Earth town not far away from Charlie and Jacke's house. Players can interact with shopkeepers and gather resources through missions given by the town mayor. Creatures have begun appearing in town following the main character's arrival on Earth. The player, with the help of Charlie and Jacke, is tasked with using their powers to protect the Earthlings downtown. In return, grateful citizens award the player with resources that the player will use to upgrade their characters.

The Haevan

This is the planet that the main character and Natalie are both from. It is a planet that also houses human life, similarly to Earth. However, there are many key differences that set it apart from Earth. The Haevan has much more extreme weather patterns than Earth, leading the chips implanted in its people to contain software to regulate body temperature automatically. The planet is also significantly smaller than Earth, leading to a much more world-wide government and community.
